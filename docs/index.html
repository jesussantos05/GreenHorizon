DESCRIPTION

Green Horizon is an RPG video game developed by 'JJKRG Studios' with the goal of raising awareness about climate change through an immersive narrative. Set in Nubrapolis, a city dominated by pollution and the corporate control of Petromal, the player takes on the role of Kael, a young man who discovers the last remnants of nature and fights to restore the lost balance. Through exploration, missions, and allies, the game encourages reflection on the environmental impact of our actions, combining entertainment and education in a unique experience.

CHARACTERISTICS

Turn-based battles

Engage in strategic battles using nature-based skills to defeat enemies corrupted by pollution and Petromal's machines.

World exploration

Travel between the polluted city of Nubrapolis and the hidden natural world, uncovering secrets, allies, and environmental truths along the way.

Player progression

Level up to improve your stats and unlock powerful nature-based abilities as you grow stronger in your fight against environmental destruction.
